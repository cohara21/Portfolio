Overview

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. A study focused on the listing flow to reduce abandonment.

The Problem

Many new sellers abandon the listing flow due to unclear steps and image upload friction.

Research & Discovery

Moderated usability tests with 10 participants, heuristic review, and analytics deep-dive.

“I didn’t know what size to choose for my item — so I skipped it.”

Process

Mapped current flows, ideated solutions, built prototypes, and validated with participants.

Solution

Streamlined form fields, added inline help, and improved image upload feedback to reduce confusion.

Results

Prototype testing indicated higher completion and fewer help requests. Next steps include measuring in production.

Reflection

Future work: broaden participant demographics and run A/B tests on inline help variants.
